The drone

Drones have the power to extend our senses and actions. Their possibilities are only limited by our imagination. The real potential of drones is as yet subject to experimentation and exploration. We believe this potential should be defined by helpfulness, modesty, and innocence.

The team

Blue Jay operates since September 2015, and currently consists of a team of 16 top students from different faculties across the Eindhoven University of Technology. Our multi-disciplinary team wants to show the potential of drones in home environments, in which they can really make a difference.
